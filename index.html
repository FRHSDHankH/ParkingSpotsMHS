Rules & Guidelines

Eligibility Requirements
  • Must be a rising senior at Marlboro High School
  • Must have a valid school ID
  • Must maintain good academic and disciplinary standing
  • Must follow all school policies and regulations
Parking Spot Rules
  • Each spot is shared between exactly two students
  • Partners must be pre-selected during registration
  • Both students can use the spot alternately or on a schedule
  • No spot transfers or changes after confirmation
  • Spots are valid for the entire school year
Important Policies
  • All vehicles must display a valid parking permit
  • No parking outside designated spots
  • No extended parking beyond school hours
  • Violations may result in loss of parking privileges
  • Report maintenance or safety issues immediately

Understanding Shared Parking Spots

What Does "Shared" Mean?

Each parking spot at Marlboro High School is designed for two students to share. This means:

  • You pick a parking partner (usually a friend or classmate)
  • Both of you can use the spot on different days
  • You arrange a schedule with your partner (e.g., alternating days)
  • The spot is reserved for both of you for the entire year
Benefits
  • More parking spots available overall
  • Flexible scheduling with your partner
  • Shared responsibility and costs
  • Social connection with parking partner
Tips
  • Choose a reliable partner
  • Establish clear schedule rules
  • Keep spot clean and respected
  • Communicate schedule changes
